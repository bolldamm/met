How to register 


****Please read these instructions carefully before clicking through to the registration form.****

Registration for METM16 is open to members and non-members alike, with different rates for members of MET, members of MET's sister associations (SfEP, EMAME, EASE, AITI and the members of the Vértice network) and non-members.

Online registration will be open until 9 October. After that date, write to Kim Eddy to find out if on-the-door registration is possible.

To register as a MET member

  • If you are a current member: Log in before submitting the conference registration form

     


    I forgot my password


  • If you were a member last year: Log in and submit a membership renewal form. Then submit the conference registration form (link below). If you can't remember your login details, click on the “I forgot my password” link or write to MET’s webmaster.
  • If you want to become a member: Submit a membership form. You will then be able to log in and submit the conference registration form (link below). 
Even if your membership has lapsed or your membership payment is pending, you will still be able to log in to our website in order to register for the conference as a member. But please note that the early-bird fee will only apply if both the membership and the conference fee are paid by the early-bird deadline.

To register as a non-member

Simply fill out and submit the conference registration form (link below).

To register as a  member of a sister association

Simply fill out and submit the conference registration form (link below). Select your association from the dropdown list in the conference registration form. MET’s sister associations are SfEP, EMAME, EASE, AITI and the members of the Vértice network.

Photo for attendee list

This year, we have created an online list of METM attendees. The list is publicly visible and will be automatically updated each time someone registers for the conference. Each person's name and conference badge details will be accompanied by a photo. If you are a MET member, your MET profile photo will be used by default, but if you wish to use a different one you can upload the photo of your choice while filling out the registration form. If you are a non-member, you will need to upload a photo. Anyone not using a MET profile photo for the attendee list should have an image file ready before continuing to the registration form. Accepted formats are .jpg, .png and .gif, and the maximum file size is 500 KB. (If you do not provide a photo, the MET logo will be displayed in its place.) 

Cancellations

If you are unable to attend for unforeseen reasons, we will process a refund if we receive written notification by 14 September 2016. All refunds will be processed within a month and will be subject to a handling fee of €35. Cancellations notified after 14 September will not qualify for a refund.
